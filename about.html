ABOUT

Taylor Sharp is a singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and producer based in Raleigh, NC. His music takes inspiration from 60's psych and chamber pop (the Beatles, the Beach Boys, the Zombies, etc.) as well as indie/alt music from the turn of the century (Belle and Sebastian, Radiohead, the Smiths). He has been releasing music since 2022, starting with the upbeat "Runaways". In 2024, he released his debut album "All of These Things", which consists of more personal, introspective songs with lush harmonies and a more diverse range of influences such as classical music, jazz, and folk.

Outside of his bedroom studio, Sharp has played local shows both solo and with his band, the Sharpies. After their debut show at Rock the Quay in 2023, Taylor Sharp and the Sharpies have played in and around the Triangle area at local venues such as Cat's Cradle Back Room, Bond Brothers Eastside, Neptunes Parlour, Slim's Tavern, and more.
